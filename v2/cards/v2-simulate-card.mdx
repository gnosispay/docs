---
title: Simulate Card Transactions
description: Simulate card transactions for testing purposes as part of sandbox environment.
---

To simulate card transactions in the sandbox environment, use our simulator API that requires authentication credentials provided by the Gnosis Pay team.

**Simulator API:** https://core.sandbox.gnosispay.in/docs/simulator-api

You can simulate card transactions using either:
- **Card ID**
- **PAN (Primary Account Number)**
